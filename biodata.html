SOME WORDS ABOUT ME

My name is Nurul Ain Binti Mohamad Salleh and I'm student from UITM Merbok, Kedah.

Let's see. If i have to describe myself in words. I would choose the word "chaotic". This is because I am the type to get confused easily and can't sit still.

Besides, my mother used to tell me that I was a naughty child. It was due to the presence of a mole behind my ear. People with a mole

behind their ears are considered naughty by the elderly, and that is precisely what happened to me.

As the second youngest kid in a household of five. That was a challenging task, in my opinion. Because the middle kid

normally does not receive as much attention as the youngest, I have already been thought to

act mischievous in attempt to win my parents' attention.
